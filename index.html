About

Evertrack Family Practice is a child-centred service committed to clarity, safety, and purposeful progress. Founded by AASW-registered social worker Andrew Macintosh, our practice delivers high-quality assessment and therapeutic services for children and families navigating complex circumstances.

Services

Family Consulting / Reg 40 Reports

We provide family assessments and family reports for court-related matters, with a focus on child development, trauma-informed practice, and balanced recommendations.

Therapeutic Services

Activity-based therapy and behaviour support for children and young people, especially those who are neurodivergent or recovering from trauma. Programs include go-karting, sim racing, bushwalking, and more.
